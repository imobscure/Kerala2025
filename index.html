A 12-Day Adventure: Dec 18th - 30th

Thursday, Dec 18

The Journey Begins

  • 21:00 - 22:40: Flight from Pune (PNQ) to Bangalore (BLR).

Friday, Dec 19

Hello Wayanad!

  • 08:50 - 10:05: Flight from Bangalore (BLR) to Kozhikode (CCJ).
  • Pick up rental car at the airport.
  • Drive to Wayanad (approx. 3-4 hours).
  • ~14:00: Check-in to hotel..

Exploring Wayanad

  • Full-day Wayanad tour using our own car.
